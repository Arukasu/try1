Search Engines

How do search engines affect our daily lives?
The search engine that the majority of the world is familiar with is Google. Google has become such an integrated part of our daily lives that we even use it as a verb. However, how can we determine what makes a browser safter than others?

The Best Privacy Search Engine

DUCKDUCKGO

No suprise to anyone, DuckDuckGo is clearly the best privacy-focused search engine. With a solid no-tracking policy, it keeps no logs on personal information. In addition, the search results are solid and are presented in a Google-type UI.

Password Managers

Password Managers are handy tools that allow the numerous passwords you have online to be saved and automatically filled in. They are an essential tool for those who frequently use the internet.

Bitwarden

Bitwarden is the password manager with a clear and concise privacy policy as well as a strong protection system.
