What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity has steadily become a relevent concern in our modern world. But what exactly does it mean?

Cybersecurity is protection of electronic devices, from webservers to cell phones, from attacks and unauthorized access.

Why does this matter to me?

As our world moves toward a future that revolves around the internet, it is important to secure sensitive data that can be found online. Consider the following information that the average user has on the internet:

  • Emails
  • Phone Numbers
  • Credit Cards and Bank Accounts
  • Current Home Address
  • Photos and Videos
  • Employment History
  • Conversations with Others
  • Social Security Number
  • Date of Birth
  • Full Legal Name
